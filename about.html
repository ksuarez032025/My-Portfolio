About Me

Front-End Developer | Syracuse, NY

Career Experience & Technical Growth

With over 14 years of experience in the financial services industry, I built my career leading card operations, payments processing, and fraud mitigation at a credit union level. As the Card Services Manager at Empower Federal Credit Union, I was responsible for overseeing Mastercard credit and debit card programs, managing processor and product conversions, and launching mobile wallet integrations like Google Pay and Samsung Pay. I played a key role in optimizing dispute workflows through Salesforce and monitoring fraud trends using Power BI dashboards.

One of my most impactful contributions came through close collaboration with our internal application services team. I served as a bridge between card operations and the technical teams — testing new workflows, validating daily file generation processes, and ensuring API integrations were mapped accurately to the correct data points. I worked hands-on with system functionality, catching errors before they reached production, and helping translate operational needs into clean technical executions.

I regularly collaborated with departments across the credit union, including accounting for chargebacks & daily settlement tracking, commercial lending (business credit cards), compliance, internal audit, and lending quality control. My passion for understanding how people interact with systems became my greatest strength: bridging human workflows with the technologies that powered them. That drive to solve operational challenges through streamlined tech solutions is what ultimately sparked my transition into coding and development.

I'm currently focused on front-end development through SheCodes, where I'm learning to build responsive, accessible websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. My past experience collaborating with departments with the financial industry taught me how to identify process pain points and work across teams to implement system-level solutions. That same mindset now drives how I approach development — with an emphasis on clean structure, usability, and cross-functional impact. From building user-first designs to integrating APIs, I'm excited to fuse my financial expertise with modern web technologies to create intuitive, scalable, and secure digital experiences for real-world operations.

Working in the fast-paced world of finance, payments, and now tech, I've come to value the importance of staying grounded. While I thrive on challenge and change, maintaining balance is essential for staying energized, focused, and at my best — for myself, my family, and my work.

They reflect the same values I bring to my work: patience, curiosity, and intention.
